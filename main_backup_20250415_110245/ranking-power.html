Improving Your Ranking

Content Quality

Focus on high-resolution streams with engaging content that keeps viewers watching until the end. Higher resolution and completion rates significantly boost your content quality score.

Engagement

Encourage discussions in your streams, respond to comments, and create content worth sharing. The engagement rate is calculated as (comments + shares*2 + reactions) divided by view count.

Consistency

Maintain a regular streaming schedule. The platform rewards creators who stream consistently rather than sporadically, even if the total hours are the same.

Ecosystem Participation

Stake STREAM tokens, participate in governance votes, and become an active community member. These actions significantly impact your overall ranking beyond just content metrics.

Ranking History

Your ranking data is updated daily. View your historical progress in the Creator Dashboard.
