What is Gematria?

Gematria is a system of assigning numerical values to letters, words, and phrases. It has been used for millennia in various mystical traditions including Jewish Kabbalah, Hermetic Qabalah, and various esoteric systems.

By converting text to numbers, practitioners believe hidden connections and meanings can be revealed through mathematical relationships.

Gematria Systems

Hebrew Gematria

The original system from Jewish mysticism that assigns specific values to each Hebrew letter.

א1 ב2 ג3 י10 ק100

English Gematria

A simple ordinal system where A=1, B=2, etc. through the English alphabet.

A1 B2 C3 Z26

Pythagorean Gematria

A reduction system where values cycle from 1-9, used in numerology.

A,J,S1 B,K,T2 I,R,Z9
