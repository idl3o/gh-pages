About StreamChain

StreamChain is a decentralized Web3 streaming platform that empowers creators and viewers by leveraging blockchain technology. Our mission is to eliminate intermediaries, provide direct monetization opportunities, and return control to content owners.

Our Vision

We envision a world where content creators can thrive without the constraints of traditional media platforms. By utilizing decentralized technology, we aim to create a fair and transparent ecosystem for all participants.

Our Values

  • Decentralization: Empowering users through distributed technology.
  • Transparency: Ensuring clear and open processes for all transactions.
  • Community: Building a supportive environment for creators and viewers alike.
