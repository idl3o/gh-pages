Governance Visualization

This page provides an overview of the governance structure of StreamChain, detailing how decisions are made and how stakeholders can participate in the governance process.

Governance Model

StreamChain employs a decentralized governance model that allows token holders to propose and vote on changes to the platform. This ensures that the community has a voice in the development and direction of the project.

Voting Mechanism

Token holders can participate in governance votes by staking their tokens. Each token represents one vote, and proposals are approved based on a majority consensus.

Participation

To participate in governance, users must hold StreamChain tokens and connect their wallets to the governance platform. Detailed instructions on how to participate will be provided in the documentation.
