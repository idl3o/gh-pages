StreamChain Token (STREAM)

The StreamChain token (STREAM) is the native utility token of the StreamChain platform, designed to facilitate transactions and incentivize participation within the ecosystem.

Purpose of the Token

The STREAM token serves multiple purposes, including:

  • Enabling seamless transactions between creators and viewers.
  • Rewarding content creators for their contributions.
  • Facilitating governance decisions within the platform.
  • Providing access to premium features and content.

Tokenomics

The total supply of STREAM tokens is capped at 1 billion tokens, distributed as follows:

  • 40% for community rewards and incentives.
  • 30% for development and operational expenses.
  • 20% for partnerships and collaborations.
  • 10% for the founding team and advisors.

How to Acquire STREAM Tokens

Users can acquire STREAM tokens through various methods, including:

  • Participating in token sales.
  • Trading on supported exchanges.
  • Receiving rewards for content creation and engagement.
