Streaming Features

StreamChain offers a range of innovative streaming features designed to enhance the experience for both creators and viewers. Our platform leverages blockchain technology to provide a decentralized and secure streaming environment.

Key Features

  • Decentralized Streaming: Enjoy uninterrupted streaming without intermediaries.
  • Direct Monetization: Creators can earn directly from their content through various revenue streams.
  • Smart Contracts: Automated payment distribution ensures transparency and fairness.
  • Community Engagement: Build and engage with your audience through interactive features.

Getting Started

To start streaming on StreamChain, creators need to set up their profiles and connect their wallets. Follow our documentation for detailed instructions.
