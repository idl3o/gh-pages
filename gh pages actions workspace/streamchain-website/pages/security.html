Security Measures

At StreamChain, we prioritize the security of our users' data and content. Our platform employs a range of security measures to ensure a safe and secure environment for all users.

Data Encryption

All user data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, utilizing industry-standard encryption protocols to protect sensitive information from unauthorized access.

Decentralized Storage

By leveraging decentralized storage solutions, we minimize the risk of data breaches and ensure that content remains accessible and secure across our network.

Regular Security Audits

Our platform undergoes regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and mitigate potential risks, ensuring that our security measures are always up to date.

User Control

Users have full control over their data and content, with the ability to manage permissions and access settings to enhance their security.
