# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: April 12, 2025*

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Web3 Crypto Streaming Service ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our decentralized platform.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Wallet addresses
- Content you create, upload, or share
- Profile information
- Communications with other users

### Information We Collect Automatically
- Usage data and analytics
- Blockchain transaction data
- Device and browser information
- Cookies and similar technologies

## How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services
- Process transactions and distribute tokens
- Verify ownership of content and NFTs
- Personalize your experience
- Communicate with you
- Comply with legal obligations

## Blockchain Data

Please be aware that information stored on public blockchains is immutable and publicly accessible. This may include:
- Wallet addresses
- Transaction details
- Content hashes and proofs
- Token ownership records

## Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:
- Access to your data
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Deletion of data (where technically possible)
- Data portability
- Restriction of processing
- Objection to processing

Note that due to the immutable nature of blockchain technology, certain rights like deletion may be limited for on-chain data.

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

## International Transfers

As a global service utilizing blockchain technology, your data may be processed in various countries with different data protection laws.

## Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at privacy@example.com.
