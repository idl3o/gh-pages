Getting Started

To begin using StreamChain, follow these steps:

  1. Create an account on the platform.
  2. Connect your cryptocurrency wallet.
  3. Explore the features available for content creators and viewers.

Features Overview

StreamChain offers a variety of features:

  • Decentralized storage for content.
  • Smart contract payments for creators.
  • Fair monetization options.
  • Creator control over content and audience.
