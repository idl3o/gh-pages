Monetization FAQ

How do creators get paid?

Payments are made directly to your connected wallet in STREAM tokens. You can keep them as STREAM tokens or convert them to other cryptocurrencies or fiat through our integrated exchange partners.

When do I receive my earnings?

Tips and one-time payments are available immediately. Subscription revenue is distributed on the last day of each month. Ad revenue is calculated and distributed by the 10th of the following month.

What are the platform fees?

Platform fees vary by monetization method, ranging from 2% for tips to 15% for ad revenue sharing. These fees cover platform maintenance, transaction costs, and ongoing development of new creator tools.

Can I combine multiple monetization methods?

Absolutely! In fact, we recommend using multiple methods to maximize your revenue. Many creators offer a mix of free content with tips, premium subscriptions, and paid special content.

How do I handle taxes on my earnings?

The platform provides downloadable earnings reports that you can use for tax reporting. We recommend consulting with a tax professional who specializes in cryptocurrency income.
