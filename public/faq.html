Frequently Asked Questions

What is StreamChain?

StreamChain is a decentralized Web3 streaming platform that empowers creators and viewers by eliminating intermediaries and enabling direct monetization.

How does the platform ensure content security?

StreamChain utilizes blockchain technology to ensure content is securely stored and distributed, providing a censorship-resistant environment for creators.

Can I connect my cryptocurrency wallet?

Yes, StreamChain allows users to connect their cryptocurrency wallets for seamless transactions and monetization options.

What types of content can I stream?

Users can stream a variety of content, including videos, live events, and more, all powered by blockchain technology.

How can I get support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, please reach out through our contact page.
